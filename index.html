Where Capital Meets Conviction

FIA Ventures is a Dubai-headquartered investment firm built for a new era of technology. We partner with visionary entrepreneurs building groundbreaking solutions that will shape tomorrow's landscape.

Beyond capital, we bring strategic mentorship, a global network of operators and co-investors, and deep domain expertise across our four core verticals. We believe the most transformative companies are cultivated through the right partnerships.
